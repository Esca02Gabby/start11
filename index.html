About Me

I'm Gabby Escamilla, I bring fresh perspective and boundless enthusiasm to UX design. With 3 years of digital media experience, I've honed my skills and developed a keen eye for crafting seamless user experiences. Currently, I thrive working directly with clients, blending creativity with practicality to bring visions to life.

When I'm not deep into crafting prototypes or immersed in project research, I enjoy a balanced blend of creativity and exploration. Whether it's sketching, watching movies, or hiking, I find joy in diverse experiences. These passions aren't just hobbies; they fuel my UX journey. Drawing sharpens my eye for detail, movies enhance my storytelling skills, and hiking teaches me about real-world navigation and user flow. In the end, these experiences intertwine seamlessly with my UX work, shaping me into a well-rounded designer with a unique perspective on crafting exceptional user experiences.

I'm also a firm believer in the power of collaboration and constant learning. Working with others allows me to see problems from different perspectives and come up with innovative solutions. I make it a priority to stay updated on industry trends and emerging technologies to ensure my designs are always relevant and impactful.
